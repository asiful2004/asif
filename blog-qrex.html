What is Qrex Server?


Qrex is a popular Minecraft server based in Bangladesh that is well-known for its PvP gameplay and exciting events. Giveaway and management of the server won the hearts of the players. If you want to play on this server, you can play with both official and unofficial accounts of Minecraft. Afnan Parvez, the founder of this server, said that it will be a server where everyone will have equal rights to play. The configuration and setup of this server are done in such a way that everyone can play on computer, mobile, console. The main objective of the Qrex team is to awaken the Minecraft community in Bangladesh in an active and new way.
